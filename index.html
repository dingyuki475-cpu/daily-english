📚 每日 5 句实用英语

2026年4月13日 · 周一
🎯 本周主题：接待外国访客
1
"Welcome to our company. Did you have a good trip?"
/ˈwelkəm tuː ˈaʊər ˈkʌmpəni. dɪd juː hæv ə ɡʊd trɪp?/
欢迎来到我们公司，旅途还顺利吗？
场景对话 · 机场迎接
You: Hi, you must be John from ABC Corp. Welcome to our company. Did you have a good trip?
John: Yes, thank you. The flight was smooth.
记忆小贴士：trip 是「旅途」，good trip 就是「旅途愉快」。这句话是接待访客的开场白，既礼貌又贴心！
2
"Let me get you a cup of coffee or tea."
/let miː ɡet juː ə kʌp əv ˈkɒfi ɔː tiː/
让我给你倒杯咖啡或茶吧。
场景对话 · 接待室
You: Please have a seat. Let me get you a cup of coffee or tea.
Guest: Coffee would be great, thank you.
记忆小贴士：get 在这里不是「得到」，而是「拿/倒」的意思。用 Let me... 开头特别客气，是招待客人的必备句！
3
"This way, please. I'll show you around."
/ðɪs weɪ pliːz. aɪl ʃəʊ juː əˈraʊnd/
请这边走，我带您参观一下。
场景对话 · 参观公司
You: This way, please. I'll show you around our office.
Guest: Wonderful, I'd love to see your workspace.
记忆小贴士：show around = 带人参观。This way, please 是引导方向的礼貌说法，配合手势更自然！
4
"Please help yourself to some refreshments."
/pliːz help jɔːself tə sʌm rɪˈfreʃmənts/
请随意享用点心饮料。
场景对话 · 会议茶歇
You: We have some snacks and drinks here. Please help yourself to some refreshments.
Guest: Thank you, everything looks delicious.
记忆小贴士：help yourself = 请自便/随意拿。refreshments 是「茶点饮料」的统称，比说 food and drinks 更正式、更地道！
5
"It was a pleasure meeting you. Safe travels!"
/ɪt wɒz ə ˈpleʒə ˈmiːtɪŋ juː. seɪf ˈtrævəlz/
很高兴见到您，一路平安！
场景对话 · 送别
You: Thank you for visiting us today. It was a pleasure meeting you. Safe travels!
Guest: Thank you for your hospitality. I look forward to our next meeting.
记忆小贴士：Safe travels 是送别时的经典祝福，比 bye bye 正式得多。hospitality = 热情款待，客人可能会用这个词夸你哦！
