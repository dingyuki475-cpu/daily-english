🎯 本周主题：职场沟通（第3天：确认）
1
"Could we confirm the meeting time for tomorrow?"
/kʊd wiː kənˈfɜːm ðə ˈmiːtɪŋ taɪm fɔː təˈmɒrəʊ/
我们可以确认一下明天的会议时间吗？
场景对话 · 确认会议安排
You: Hi, this is Amy from ABC Trading. Could we confirm the meeting time for tomorrow?
Client: Yes, it's still at 10 AM. Should we meet at the lobby?
记忆小贴士：confirm = 确认。职场必用！会议前、约定前都应确认，避免误会。开头加 "Could we" 更礼貌。
2
"I just wanted to double-check the delivery date with you."
/aɪ dʒʌst ˈwɒntɪd tuː ˈdʌbl tʃek ðə ˈdelɪvəri deɪt wɪð juː/
我想和您再次确认一下交货日期。
场景对话 · 确认订单交期
You: Hi Mark, I just wanted to double-check the delivery date with you.
Mark: Sure, it's scheduled for May 15th. Is that still okay for you?
记忆小贴士：double-check = 再确认/核对。"just wanted to" 让语气更柔和，不显得咄咄逼人。
3
"Can I confirm your email address for the quotation?"
/ken aɪ kənˈfɜːm jɔː ˈiːmeɪl əˈdres fɔː ðə kwəʊˈteɪʃn/
我可以确认一下您的邮箱地址，以便发送报价单吗？
场景对话 · 确认邮件信息
You: Before I send the quotation, can I confirm your email address?
Client: Yes, it's contact at companyname dot com.
记忆小贴士：quotation = 报价单。外贸必备！发报价、合同前必确认邮箱，避免发错地址。
4
"I want to verify that we've received your payment."
/aɪ wɒnt tuː ˈverɪfaɪ ðæt wiːv rɪˈsiːvd jɔː ˈpeɪmənt/
我想确认一下我们是否收到了您的付款。
场景对话 · 确认付款到账
You: Hello, I want to verify that we've received your payment.
Client: Yes, I transferred it this morning. You should have received it.
记忆小贴士：verify = 核实/验证。"want to verify" 比 "I need to check" 听起来更专业！
5
"Let me just confirm the details before we wrap up."
/let miː dʒʌst kənˈfɜːm ðə ˈdiːteɪlz bɪˈfɔː wiː ræp ʌp/
在我们结束之前，让我确认一下细节。
场景对话 · 会议结尾总结确认
You: Let me just confirm the details before we wrap up. So we're agreed on the sample date and the final delivery?
Client: Yes, that's correct. Thank you for the clear summary.
记忆小贴士：wrap up = 结束/收尾。"before we wrap up" 是会议结尾确认的完美开场白！
