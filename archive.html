SENTENCE 01
"I am writing to follow up on my previous email."
📖 我写信是为了跟进我之前发送的邮件。
🔊 /aɪ æm ˈraɪtɪŋ tuː ˈfɒləʊ ʌp ɒn maɪ ˈpriːviəs ˈiːmeɪl/
💬 场景对话 · 催回复
A：Hi David, I am writing to follow up on my previous email about the shipment schedule.
B：Oh sorry, I got caught up. I'll get back to you today.
"follow up" = 跟进，"I am writing to..." 是写邮件的万能开头！
SENTENCE 02
"Please find the attached file for your reference."
📖 请查看附件，供您参考。
🔊 /pliːz faɪnd ðə əˈtæʧt faɪl fɔː jɔː ˈrefrəns/
💬 场景对话 · 发报价单
A：Hi Sarah, please find the attached file for your reference. It includes our latest price list.
B：Thank you! I'll review it and get back to you soon.
"Please find the attached..." 是发附件的标准句，比 "I attached..." 更正式！
SENTENCE 03
"Could you please confirm receipt of this email?"
📖 您能确认已收到这封邮件吗？
🔊 /kʊd juː pliːz kənˈfɜːm rɪˈsiːt ɒv ðɪs ˈiːmeɪl/
💬 场景对话 · 确认收件
A：Hi Tom, I sent you the contract yesterday. Could you please confirm receipt of this email?
B：Yes, I received it. I'm reviewing it now.
receipt 中 p 不发音，读 /rɪˈsiːt/，注意别读成 re-CEIPT！
SENTENCE 04
"I apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused."
📖 对此可能给您带来的不便，我深感抱歉。
🔊 /aɪ əˈpɒlədʒaɪz fɔː ˈeni ɪnkənˈviːniəns ðɪs meɪ həv kɔːzd/
💬 场景对话 · 延误道歉
A：The shipment was delayed due to port congestion. I apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused.
B：I understand. Please keep me updated on the new arrival date.
外贸道歉邮件"黄金句"，背下来能处理80%的延误投诉！
SENTENCE 05
"I look forward to hearing from you soon."
📖 期待您的早日回复。
🔊 /aɪ lʊk ˈfɔːwəd tuː ˈhɪərɪŋ frɒm juː suːn/
💬 场景对话 · 邮件结尾
A：Please let me know if you have any questions. I look forward to hearing from you soon.
B：Sure, I'll get back to you by end of day.
邮件结尾万能句！"look forward to + doing" 固定搭配，后面接动词-ing形式。

🛒 日常生活 · 社交场景 · 第3周第7天
▼
SENTENCE 01
"It's so great to finally meet you in person!"
📖 终于见到您本人，真的太高兴了！
🔊 /ɪts səʊ ɡreɪt tuː ˈfaɪnəli miːt juː ɪn ˈpɜːsn/
💬 场景对话 · 初次见面
A：It's so great to finally meet you in person! I've heard so much about you.
B：Likewise! I've been looking forward to this meeting.
"in person" = 亲自/当面，和网络/电话交流相对，是初次见面的热情表达！
SENTENCE 02
"Would you like me to take a photo for you?"
📖 需要我帮您拍张照片吗？
🔊 /wʊd juː laɪk miː tuː teɪk ə ˈfəʊtəʊ fɔː juː/
💬 场景对话 · 景点参观
A：Would you like me to take a photo for you? The view here is amazing.
B：Oh yes, please! That would be wonderful, thank you.
"Would you like me to..." = 需要我帮您...吗？比 "Do you want me to..." 更礼貌！
SENTENCE 03
"Please feel free to let me know if you need anything."
📖 如有任何需要，请随时告诉我。
🔊 /pliːz fiːl friː tuː let miː nəʊ ɪf juː niːd ˈeniθɪŋ/
💬 场景对话 · 陪同服务
A：Please feel free to let me know if you need anything during your stay.
B：Thank you so much, you've been very helpful!
"feel free to" = 随意/不用客气，接待客人时超好用的礼貌短语！
SENTENCE 04
"Do you have any dietary restrictions I should know about?"
📖 您有什么饮食禁忌或忌口吗？
🔊 /duː juː həv ˈeni ˈdaɪətri rɪˈstrɪkʃənz aɪ ʃʊd nəʊ əˈbaʊt/
💬 场景对话 · 安排餐厅
A：We've reserved a table at a local restaurant. Do you have any dietary restrictions I should know about?
B：I'm vegetarian, so no meat please.
"dietary restrictions" = 饮食限制/忌口，接待外国客人必问！避免尴尬场面。
SENTENCE 05
"It was a pleasure having you here. Safe travels!"
📖 很高兴您来访，祝一路顺风！
🔊 /ɪt wɒz ə ˈpleʒər ˈhævɪŋ juː hɪər. seɪf ˈtrævlz/
💬 场景对话 · 送别
A：It was a pleasure having you here. Safe travels, and hope to see you again soon!
B：Thank you for everything, I really enjoyed the visit!
"Safe travels!" = 一路平安！是送别外国朋友最地道的说法，比"bye"有温度多了！

📊 职场沟通 · 汇报篇 · 第1周第5天
▼
SENTENCE 01
"I'd like to give you a quick update on the project."
📖 我想给您做一个项目的简要汇报。
🔊 /aɪd laɪk tuː ɡɪv juː ə kwɪk ˈʌpdeɪt ɒn ðə ˈprɒdʒekt/
💬 场景对话 · 开始汇报
A：Good morning everyone. I'd like to give you a quick update on the project.
B：Please go ahead.
"a quick update" = 简要汇报，是会议开场的万能句，记住就能流畅开口！
SENTENCE 02
"We have completed about 70% of the work so far."
📖 到目前为止，我们已完成了约70%的工作。
🔊 /wiː həv kəmˈpliːtɪd əˈbaʊt ˈsevnti pɜːsent ɒv ðə wɜːk səʊ fɑː/
💬 场景对话 · 进度汇报
A：We have completed about 70% of the work so far, and we are on schedule.
B：Great, keep up the good work!
"so far" = 到目前为止，"on schedule" = 按计划进行，两个词组都超常用！
SENTENCE 03
"The main challenge we are facing is the tight deadline."
📖 我们面临的主要挑战是交期紧张。
🔊 /ðə meɪn ˈʧælɪndʒ wiː ɑː ˈfeɪsɪŋ ɪz ðə taɪt ˈdedlaɪn/
💬 场景对话 · 提出问题
A：The main challenge we are facing is the tight deadline. We may need extra support.
B：I understand. What kind of support do you need?
"tight deadline" = 紧张的截止日期，外贸常用！tight = 紧的/紧张的。
SENTENCE 04
"I suggest we arrange an extra shift to speed things up."
📖 我建议我们安排加班来加快进度。
🔊 /aɪ səˈdʒest wiː əˈreɪndʒ ən ˈekstrə ʃɪft tuː spiːd θɪŋz ʌp/
💬 场景对话 · 提出建议
A：I suggest we arrange an extra shift to speed things up and meet the deadline.
B：Good idea. Please coordinate with the factory immediately.
"speed things up" = 加快进度，"arrange a shift" = 安排班次/加班，工厂沟通常用！
SENTENCE 05
"Thank you for your time. Any questions before we wrap up?"
📖 感谢您的时间，结束前有什么问题吗？
🔊 /θæŋk juː fɔː jɔː taɪm. ˈeni ˈkwesʧənz bɪˈfɔː wiː ræp ʌp/
💬 场景对话 · 会议结尾
A：That's all from my side. Thank you for your time. Any questions before we wrap up?
B：No questions from me. Good job, everyone!
"wrap up" = 结束/收尾，是会议和汇报的完美结束语！

✅ 职场沟通 · 确认篇 · 第1周第3天

SENTENCE 01
"Just to confirm, the meeting is at 3 PM tomorrow, right?"
📖 确认一下，会议是明天下午3点，对吗？
🔊 /dʒʌst tuː kənˈfɜːm, ðə ˈmiːtɪŋ ɪz ət θriː piː em təˈmɒrəʊ raɪt/
💬 场景对话 · 确认时间
A：Just to confirm, the meeting is at 3 PM tomorrow, right?
B：That's correct. I'll send you the meeting link shortly.
"Just to confirm..." = 只是确认一下...，语气轻松不失礼，避免误会的万能开头！
SENTENCE 02
"Could you please confirm the delivery date for this order?"
📖 能否请您确认一下这笔订单的交货日期？
🔊 /kʊd juː pliːz kənˈfɜːm ðə dɪˈlɪvəri deɪt fɔː ðɪs ˈɔːdər/
💬 场景对话 · 确认交期
A：Could you please confirm the delivery date for this order? Our client is asking.
B：The goods will be ready by June 10th.
"delivery date" = 交货日期，外贸高频词！和工厂、客户沟通必用。
SENTENCE 03
"Please let me know if I have the right email address for you."
📖 如果我的邮箱地址有误，请告知。
🔊 /pliːz let miː nəʊ ɪf aɪ həv ðə raɪt ˈiːmeɪl əˈdres fɔː juː/
💬 场景对话 · 确认邮箱
A：I'll send you the quotation shortly. Please let me know if I have the right email address for you.
B：Yes, this is correct. Thank you.
这句话体现细心和专业，给客户留下好印象！初次合作时特别有用。
SENTENCE 04
"I'd like to confirm that the payment has been received."
📖 我想确认一下款项已经收到。
🔊 /aɪd laɪk tuː kənˈfɜːm ðæt ðə ˈpeɪmənt həz biːn rɪˈsiːvd/
💬 场景对话 · 确认收款
A：I'd like to confirm that the payment has been received. Could you send us the bank receipt?
B：Of course, I'll send you the wire transfer receipt right away.
"payment received" = 款项已收到，外贸财务确认核心句！
SENTENCE 05
"So we are all on the same page — the deadline is Friday."
📖 那我们达成共识——截止日期是周五。
🔊 /səʊ wiː ɑː ɔːl ɒn ðə seɪm peɪdʒ ðə ˈdedlaɪn ɪz ˈfraɪdeɪ/
💬 场景对话 · 会议收尾确认
A：Great discussion everyone. So we are all on the same page — the deadline is Friday.
B：Confirmed. We'll make sure everything is ready.
"on the same page" = 达成共识/想法一致，会议常用地道表达！

🤝 接待外国访客 · 第2周第2天
▼
SENTENCE 01
"How was your flight? I hope it wasn't too tiring."
📖 您的航班怎么样？希望不太累。
🔊 /haʊ wɒz jɔː flaɪt? aɪ həʊp ɪt wɒznt tuː ˈtaɪərɪŋ/
💬 场景对话 · 机场接机
A：Welcome! How was your flight? I hope it wasn't too tiring.
B：It was a long journey, but I'm glad to be here!
"How was your flight?" 是接机必问句！关心对方旅途，暖场效果极佳。
SENTENCE 02
"The hotel has everything you need — feel free to freshen up."
📖 酒店设施齐全，您可以先休息梳洗一下。
🔊 /ðə həʊˈtel həz ˈevriθɪŋ juː niːd, fiːl friː tuː ˈfreʃn ʌp/
💬 场景对话 · 安排入住
A：The hotel has everything you need. Feel free to freshen up, and we'll meet in the lobby at 6.
B：Perfect, thank you for arranging everything.
"freshen up" = 梳洗/整理一下，比说"go wash up"更礼貌自然！
SENTENCE 03
"Is there anything else I can help you with?"
📖 还有什么我能帮您的吗？
🔊 /ɪz ðeər ˈeniθɪŋ els aɪ kən help juː wɪð/
💬 场景对话 · 全程陪同
A：We've checked you in. Is there anything else I can help you with?
B：Could you recommend a good pharmacy nearby?
这句话随时可用，是贴心服务的标志句，让客人感受到被照顾！
SENTENCE 04
"I've booked a table at a restaurant known for local specialties."
📖 我已在一家以当地特色菜著称的餐厅订好了位置。
🔊 /aɪv bʊkt ə ˈteɪbl ət ə ˈrestrɒnt nəʊn fɔː ˈləʊkl speʃˈælɪtɪz/
💬 场景对话 · 商务宴请
A：I've booked a table at a restaurant known for local specialties. I hope you'll enjoy it.
B：That sounds wonderful! I love trying local food.
"local specialties" = 当地特色菜，带外国客人吃饭时的好表达！
SENTENCE 05
"We've arranged a car to take you to the airport tomorrow morning."
📖 我们已安排好明早的车送您去机场。
🔊 /wiːv əˈreɪndʒd ə kɑː tuː teɪk juː tuː ðə ˈeəpɔːt təˈmɒrəʊ ˈmɔːnɪŋ/
💬 场景对话 · 送别安排
A：We've arranged a car to take you to the airport tomorrow morning. It'll pick you up at 7.
B：Thank you so much for everything!
"arrange a car" = 安排用车，外贸接待常见场景。比说"hire a taxi"更正式！

🤝 接待外国访客 · 第2周第1天（首日）
▼
SENTENCE 01
"Welcome to our company. Did you have a good trip?"
📖 欢迎来到我们公司，旅途还顺利吗？
🔊 /ˈwelkəm tuː ˈaʊər ˈkʌmpəni. dɪd juː həv ə ɡʊd trɪp/
💬 场景对话 · 公司迎接
A：Welcome to our company! Did you have a good trip?
B：Yes, thank you! It was a smooth journey.
"Did you have a good trip?" 是迎接宾客必备句，简单自然，对方一定听得懂！
SENTENCE 02
"Please help yourself to some tea or coffee."
📖 请随意喝茶或咖啡。
🔊 /pliːz help jɔːˈself tuː sʌm tiː ɔː ˈkɒfi/
💬 场景对话 · 待茶待客
A：Please have a seat. Please help yourself to some tea or coffee.
B：Thank you, I'll have some tea please.
"help yourself to..." = 请随意取用，比 "please take" 更地道热情！
SENTENCE 03
"Let me show you around our factory."
📖 让我带您参观一下我们的工厂。
🔊 /let miː ʃəʊ juː əˈraʊnd ˈaʊər ˈfæktri/
💬 场景对话 · 工厂参观
A：Let me show you around our factory. We'll start with the production line.
B：Great, I'm looking forward to seeing your operation.
"show around" = 带领参观，是陪访客游览的核心句，简单好用！
SENTENCE 04
"This is where we handle all the quality control."
📖 这里是我们进行所有质量控制的地方。
🔊 /ðɪs ɪz weər wiː ˈhændl ɔːl ðə ˈkwɒlɪti kənˈtrəʊl/
💬 场景对话 · 参观介绍
A：This is where we handle all the quality control. Every product is checked before shipping.
B：Very impressive! How many inspectors do you have?
"quality control" = 质量控制/品控，外贸高频词，简称QC！
SENTENCE 05
"It's been a pleasure. We hope to see you again soon."
📖 很荣幸，希望很快再见到您。
🔊 /ɪts biːn ə ˈpleʒər. wiː həʊp tuː siː juː əˈɡen suːn/
💬 场景对话 · 送别
A：It's been a pleasure working with you. We hope to see you again soon!
B：Likewise! This has been a very productive visit.
"It's been a pleasure." 是道别时最经典的一句话，有礼有温度！
